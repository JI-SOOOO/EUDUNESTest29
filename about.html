About



Mission


❝We aim to grow every year stronger with our services providing
a top class quality to our partners and clients.❞


We dream to build our company with solid roots and to always respect our values. We thrive to work with like-minded partners that appreciate our company culture and trust us with developing their goals and projects.




Vision



In our future we want to become great connectors of East and West and strive to be a recognizable and respectable name that will be distinguished for the care and respect of our company value including sustainability, gender equality and diversity and above all honesty.




Our Value



We believe in professionalism, strong care and understanding of clients needs, flexibility, creativity and a modern and feminine touch to solve all project issues. We also truly care for sustainibility in all its formss, both environmentally but also socially, deeply thriving to achieve gender equality, corporate diversity representation and equality. This is why our team is female-led, diverse and multicultural.






Our story

Marta and Rossella started the business in 2020 to follow their need of creating their own company based on their experience and values. They follow their intuition and passion and that is how Eudunes came to be a bridge between East and West, Europe and Asia. Our co-founders put their whole expertise and passion in every project and want to progress with their clients with professionality and deep understanding.
